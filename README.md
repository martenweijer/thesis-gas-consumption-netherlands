# Predicting Natural Gas Consumption in the Netherlands

Master's thesis research predicting residential natural gas consumption at the municipal level in the Netherlands using machine learning.

## Research Question

How accurately can machine learning models predict residential natural gas consumption at the municipal level in the Netherlands?

**Sub-questions:**
1. What socio-demographic, economic, and housing characteristics are most predictive of municipal-level residential gas consumption?
2. Which machine learning algorithms perform best, and how do they compare to traditional statistical methods?
3. How does model performance vary across municipality types (urban vs. rural, high vs. low income)?
